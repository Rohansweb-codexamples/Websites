Build Your Site with Google Sites

Simple, drag-and-drop, and no coding required.

1

Access Sites

Go to sites.google.com and sign in with your Google account. Click the "+" button to start a blank site or pick a template.

2

Design Your Layout

Use the "Insert" panel on the right to add text, images, and embedded links. You can drag these elements around to organize your page.

3

Publish

When you're happy, click the purple "Publish" button. You'll choose a web address suffix, and your site will be live instantly.
